Monster Energy is one of the most influential and quintessential energy drink brands in the world with its unique green-claw logo recognized by people far and wide. It is a globally recognized brand that is most notably known for the sponsorship and support of sporting events such as motocross, skateboarding, snowboarding, MMA, BMX, and other such extreme sports

Project summary

By Jamie Harmon

I wanted to use this page to delcare some of the areas of this project that I believe I undertook further understanding.


1. Use of Github to archive the project, the github link can be found via my submission on canvas and by clicking the github logo within my footer.


2. I managed to create a workflow from photoshop creating a texture sheet, to blender, exporting to an fbx, fbx loading into 3DS max to add cameras and lights, then exporting into VRML, then converting to VRML into a usable X3D file.


3. Additional modelling research, alot of the time I spent on this project unfortunately didn't amount to much usable, but I did realise how powerful the potential for X3D can be when I saw blender could intake OBJ files. I made use of this by using a third party client that I use to play the online MMO runescape, having a plugin export in-game models into blender. I was then able to make use of my previous workflow to get the model into the site as seen below, all without having a texture manually attached to it.


Overall I did find the project very enjoyable even if it was extrmely challenging for me and I did not accomplish all I had hoped to, thankyou for taking the module it was very thorough.
